## 1. 项目概述
- **项目名称**：RoboHorse
- **项目简介**：
迎来到Robohorse,这是一个激动人心的新项目,将科学家和他们的粉丝联系在一起。

在Robohorse,科学家可以创建自己的粉丝NFT,这些NFT会包含该科学家独特的机器人脚本和算法。粉丝可以购买这些科学家铸造的粉丝NFT,获得该科学家机器人作品的所有权和使用权。

作为平台,我们为科学家提供发行粉丝NFT的工具和渠道。一旦粉丝获得了科学家的粉丝NFT,他们就可以通过我们的执行引擎运行该科学家的机器人脚本,实现特定的机器人交互和体验。

Robohorse旨在连接科学家和他们的粉丝群体,建立一个共同推动机器人发展的社区。通过收集科学家粉丝NFT,粉丝可以加入这个DAO组织,参与社区治理,支持科学家继续创作。

加入Robohorse,随着科学家一起抢占机器人的制高点,成为这个革命性社区的一员!

Welcome to Robohorse, an exciting new project that connects scientists and their fans.
On Robohorse, scientists can create their own fan NFTs containing unique robot scripts and algorithms from that scientist. Fans can purchase these scientist-minted fan NFTs to gain ownership and usage rights to the scientist's robotic works.
As a platform, we provide scientists with the tools and channels to issue fan NFTs. Once fans acquire a scientist's fan NFT, they can run the scientist's robot scripts through our execution engine to enable specific robotic interactions and experiences.
Robohorse aims to connect scientists with their fan communities, building a collective that pushes robotics forward together. By collecting scientist fan NFTs, fans can join this DAO organization, participate in community governance, and support scientists to keep creating.
Join Robohorse, stake your claim in robotics alongside scientists, and become part of this groundbreaking community!
